Everything happens automatically

No timeline. No editing. Just record and go.

Speed Ramping

Idle moments get sped up 4-8x automatically. Active parts stay at normal speed. Your 30s recording becomes a tight 15s clip.

🔍

Auto Zoom

Clicks trigger smooth zoom-ins with spring physics. The camera follows your cursor cinematically, then eases back out.

🖱️

Cursor Rendering

Your cursor is re-rendered with perfect sync. Click highlights show exactly where you clicked with an expanding ring effect.

👻

Invisible App

No window clutter. Lives in your menu bar. Double-tap Control to record, double-tap to stop. That's it.

📋

Instant Clipboard

Finished recording saves to your clipboard automatically. Paste directly into Slack, Discord, or anywhere.

🎵

Sound Effects

Subtle audio blips confirm recording start and stop. A floating pill indicator shows you're recording with a live timer.
